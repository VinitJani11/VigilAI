How to Read These Results
Grad-CAM

Gradient Class Activation Map

Shows which spatial regions had the highest influence on the detection decision. Hot/bright areas = the AI focused most here. Computed by weighting activation regions by detection confidence.

LIME

Local Interpretable Model-agnostic

Segments the image into superpixels and systematically hides each region to measure its contribution. Green = helped detection. Works with any ML model without accessing internals.

Combined

Interpreting Together

When Grad-CAM hot regions and LIME green superpixels overlap, that region is both strongly activated and positively contributing — giving you high confidence the AI is focusing on the right thing.
